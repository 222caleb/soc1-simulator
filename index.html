Key findings

8/8
IPs attacked in perfect synchronization
All 8 distinct source IPs began their attack at exactly 17:39:17 and ceased within the same 1-second window which is a clear botnet coordination signature.
7
Countries across 4 continents
Attack infrastructure spanned Singapore, United States, Indonesia, Germany, Lithuania, Nigeria, and Russia, consistent with distributed botnet operations.
100%
Detection rate, zero successful compromises
Despite 4,000 attack attempts across the campaign, no external IP successfully authenticated. All defenses held.
T1110.001
MITRE ATT&CK technique mapped
Attacks mapped to Password Guessing (T1110.001) primary and Credential Stuffing (T1110.004) secondary, based on target username patterns.
15
Service accounts targeted
Credential stuffing focused on common Linux service accounts: root, admin, mysql, postgres, oracle, jenkins, www-data, git, deploy. None exist on the target.
